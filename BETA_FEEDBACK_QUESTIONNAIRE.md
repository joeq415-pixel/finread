# FinRead Beta Feedback Questionnaire

Use this to create a Google Form for beta tester feedback.

## Questions to Include:

### 1. Overall Satisfaction
**Question Type:** Linear Scale (1-5)
- **Question:** "How satisfied are you with FinRead overall?"
- **Scale:** 1 (Not Satisfied) to 5 (Very Satisfied)

### 2. Key Features
**Question Type:** Multiple Choice
- **Question:** "Which features did you find most valuable?"
- **Options:**
  - AI-Powered Analysis of SEC filings
  - Plain-English Explanations
  - Ask AI Questions about filings
  - Watchlist functionality
  - Financial metrics and insights
  - All features equally useful
  - None particularly useful

### 3. Feature Improvement
**Question Type:** Multiple Choice (Select all that apply)
- **Question:** "Which features would you like to see improved or added?"
- **Options:**
  - Better mobile experience
  - More filing types (international)
  - Comparison tools between companies
  - Export/download functionality
  - Integration with other platforms
  - Faster analysis time
  - Better UI/UX
  - Other (please specify)

### 4. Pricing Feedback
**Question Type:** Multiple Choice
- **Question:** "What do you think about our Pro pricing of $4.99/month?"
- **Options:**
  - Too expensive
  - Reasonably priced
  - Great value
  - Would prefer yearly discount option
  - Would prefer higher tier option
  - Unsure

### 5. Alternative Pricing
**Question Type:** Short Answer
- **Question:** "If you selected 'Too expensive' above, what price point would you consider fair?"
- **Placeholder:** "e.g., $2.99/month, $9.99/month, etc."

### 6. Likelihood to Recommend
**Question Type:** Linear Scale (1-10)
- **Question:** "How likely are you to recommend FinRead to others?"
- **Scale:** 1 (Not Likely) to 10 (Very Likely)

### 7. Use Case
**Question Type:** Short Answer
- **Question:** "How do you plan to use FinRead? (e.g., personal investing, financial analysis, research, etc.)"

### 8. Additional Feedback
**Question Type:** Paragraph/Long Answer
- **Question:** "What's one thing we should know about your experience with FinRead?"
- **Placeholder:** "Share any additional thoughts, bugs, or suggestions..."

### 9. Contact (Optional)
**Question Type:** Short Answer
- **Question:** "Can we contact you for follow-up questions? If yes, provide your email."

---

## How to Create in Google Forms:

1. Go to **forms.google.com**
2. Click **"Create new form"**
3. Title: "FinRead Beta Feedback"
4. Add each question above
5. Click **"Share"** (top right)
6. Copy the link
7. Paste into the newsletter

---

## Newsletter Call-to-Action:

Add this section before the footer:

```
Help Us Improve!
We'd love your feedback on your FinRead experience.
Take 2 minutes to answer our quick survey.

[TAKE SURVEY BUTTON] → Google Form Link
```

---

## Analyzing Responses:

After sending:
1. Go to your Google Form
2. Click **"Responses"** tab
3. See summary statistics
4. Download as CSV for deeper analysis
5. Track:
   - Satisfaction scores
   - Pricing feedback
   - Feature requests
   - NPS (Net Promoter Score) from question 6
