Our Story

Born in the
Sunset District

Bobaville started in 2018 as a tiny window shop on Irving Street. We were obsessed with one thing: making boba that actually tasted like something. No powders. No shortcuts. Just hand-brewed tea, real fruit, and love.

Six years later, we've grown to three SF locations — but every drink is still made the same way. Because you deserve something beautiful in your cup.
